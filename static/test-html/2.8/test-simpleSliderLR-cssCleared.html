Item description:

It is made from rare gold boa scales and golden silk. It is indestructible. There is a legend in the West Regions that this item is of divine origin. Long ago, though, the Raiment was lost in the Central Plains and has not been seen for many years.

Directions:

Purchase a Golden Snake Gift Bouquet. Use it to obtain the Lore “Gold Snake Raiment” and a headwear diagram. The headwear diagram is bound and permanent. The equipment modified by the drawing will also be bound.The Gold Snake Raiment has the Lore Skill “Starry Sky.” This will give the character an eye-catching fireworks aura!

Features
The Gold Snake Raiment is the first gold clothing set in Age of Wushu.
The headwear comes with the set but can be worn with any other set. Players can mix and match as they like.
Wearing gold clothes in the autumn is auspicious and can bring the player good luck!
